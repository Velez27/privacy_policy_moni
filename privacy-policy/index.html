Privacy Policy

My Personal Finances — by Desvez

Effective date: February 12, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to My Personal Finances ("the App"), developed by Desvez. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our mobile application. By using the App you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

The types of information we collect depend on how you use the App:

Data Type Source Details
Email & display name Google OAuth sign-in Collected only when you sign in with your Google account
Financial data User input Transactions, categories, recurring rules, savings goals, currency preferences, and balances
Device language Device settings Used to set the default app language (Spanish or English)
Ad identifiers Google Mobile Ads SDK Device advertising ID, IP address, and device information — collected only for non-premium users when ads are displayed

3. Guest Mode

The App offers a Guest Mode that does not require any sign-in. When using Guest Mode:

  • No personal information (email, name) is collected.
  • All financial data is stored locally on your device only.
  • No data is synced to the cloud.

4. How We Use Your Data

  • App functionality: To provide and maintain the core features of the App, including tracking transactions, managing categories, and processing recurring rules.
  • Cloud sync: For premium users, to synchronize financial data across sessions via our cloud service (Supabase).
  • Ads: For free users, to display personalized advertisements through Google Mobile Ads (AdMob).
  • Reports: To generate financial reports — all report processing happens locally on your device and is not uploaded to any server.

5. Data Storage & Security

Local Storage

All users' data is stored on-device using AsyncStorage. This data remains on your device and is not accessible to us unless you are a premium user with cloud sync enabled.

Cloud Storage (Premium Users Only)

Premium users' data is stored in a Supabase-hosted PostgreSQL database protected by Row Level Security (RLS), ensuring that each user can only access their own data. All communication between the App and our servers uses HTTPS encryption.

Authentication Tokens

OAuth tokens are stored securely on your device and are never transmitted to third parties beyond the authentication provider.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data to any third party.

Your data is shared only with the following services as necessary to operate the App:

  • Google — For authentication (OAuth) and advertisement delivery (AdMob).
  • Supabase — For cloud data synchronization (premium users only).

8. Your Rights & Data Deletion

  • You can use Guest Mode at any time to avoid all personal data collection.
  • You can request the deletion of all your data stored in our cloud systems by contacting us at desvez@gmail.com.
  • Local data on your device can be deleted by clearing the App's data or uninstalling the App.

9. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe that a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, please contact us at desvez@gmail.com so we can take appropriate action.
