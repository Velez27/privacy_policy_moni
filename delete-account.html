How to Request Account Deletion

If you would like to delete your account and all associated data from My Personal Finances, please follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to desvez@gmail.com with the subject line: "Account Deletion Request - My Personal Finances".
  2. Include the email address associated with your Google account used to sign in to the app.
  3. We will process your request and permanently delete all your data within 30 days.
  4. You will receive a confirmation email once the deletion is complete.

Guest users: If you use the app in Guest Mode, no data is stored on our servers. You can delete all local data by clearing the app's data in your device settings or by uninstalling the app.

Data That Will Be Deleted

Upon processing your deletion request, the following data will be permanently removed from our servers:

Data Type Action
User profile (email, display name) Permanently deleted
Transactions Permanently deleted
Categories Permanently deleted
Recurring rules Permanently deleted
Premium purchase records Permanently deleted
Data stored locally on your device Not affected — clear app data or uninstall to remove

Retention Period

No data is retained after the deletion is processed. All cloud-stored data is permanently deleted within 30 days of receiving your request. No backups or copies are kept after this period.

Contact

If you have any questions about the deletion process, please contact us at desvez@gmail.com.
